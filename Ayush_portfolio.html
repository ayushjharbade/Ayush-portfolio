About me

Media instinct meets digital strategy

I'm a Social Media Executive at Navbharat, contributing to the digital presence of one of Nagpur's most trusted news platforms. My work sits at the intersection of journalism, storytelling, and platform strategy.

What makes my approach different: I don't just post content — I think about why a story matters, who needs to hear it, and how to frame it so it actually reaches people.
